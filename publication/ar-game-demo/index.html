Jumping Rabbits! -- an AR shooting game taking place on real-world planes

Jundan Luo
June 2017
Video
Rabbits are jumping on the real planes: [video]Jumping rabbits!

Abstract

The augmented reality shooting game took place in multiple planar regions in the real world. Move your phone to watch the rabbits and shoot!
A SLAM SDK was used to estimate the camera pose. The real-world 3D point cloud was reconstructed by stereo-matching, and I used an image-based segmentation algorithm to extract planes. The game was rendered by Unity3D.
